## Pricing

**Research:** Dubai market benchmarked — websites AED 8K–40K, chatbots AED 14K–30K, voice AED 8K–90K, monthly retainers AED 6K–15K.

**Final 5-tier structure:**

| Tier | AED/mo | Commitment |
|---|---|---|
| Launch | 1,200 | Month-to-month |
| Spark | 3,000 | Month-to-month |
| Foundation | 6,000 | 3 months min |
| Growth | 14,000 | 6 months min |
| Scale | 32,000 | 12 months min |

**Launch tier** added specifically for young ecom / first Shopify stores / dropshippers.  
**Shopify Starter Pack:** AED 4,500 flat (store + brand kit + chatbot + 1 month Launch free).  
**Scale not sold until Month 3** — SOPs must be locked first.

**Payment options:** Monthly (standard) · Installment +5% · Annual prepay −10% · One-time builds.  
**Onboarding fees:** AED 500 (Launch/Spark) · AED 1,500 (Foundation+). Pause: 1×/year, max 1 month.  
**Commission split:** 4% workers · 32% × 3 partners. API cost buffer: 20%.

**PDFs built:** v1 → v2 → Final → Final_2 → **Nova_Agency_Pricing_Branded.pdf** (final — full Nova branding, correct prices, all payment options).
