---

## Launch — AED 1,200/mo

Entry level, no commitment. Built for young entrepreneurs and first-time store owners who need one service running professionally without a long contract.

**Best for:** first-time Shopify owners, dropshippers testing their first product, solo founders who only need one channel managed.

**What you get:** one focused channel (Instagram or TikTok) handled end-to-end.

**Includes:**

- Pick any 1 service from the catalog
- 10 posts/mo + AI captions + hashtag research
- 1 short-form video/mo (Reel or TikTok)
- 5 product page descriptions/mo
- Monthly performance report
- WhatsApp support (business hours)
- 48-hour onboarding · AED 500 one-time
- Cancel anytime

---

## Spark — AED 3,000/mo

Everything in Launch plus a second platform and the basics of engagement. Less than a business lunch in Dubai per day, still month-to-month.

**Best for:** early-stage ecom stores testing two channels, F&B brands ready to be on IG and TikTok, service businesses moving past "post when I remember."

**What you get:** 2 platforms managed, 15 posts/mo, basic comment + DM responses, monthly strategy call.

**Includes (everything in Launch, plus):**

- 2nd platform added (IG · TikTok · X · FB · Snapchat)
- 15 posts/mo with AI captions and hashtags
- Basic comment and DM responses
- Monthly strategy call (30 min)
- 48-hour onboarding · AED 500 one-time
- Cancel anytime

---

## Foundation — AED 6,000/mo

Most popular tier. The inflection point where AI starts compounding. Cheaper than hiring one social media manager (AED 8,000–12,000/mo salary).

**Best for:** growing ecom brands ready for consistent output, clinics and salons that need an after-hours chatbot, B2B service businesses wanting a real content engine.

**What you get:** 3 platforms, 20 posts/mo, 4 short-form videos/mo, AI content engine, basic web chatbot, email flows + weekly broadcast, monthly strategy call.

**Includes (everything in Spark, plus):**

- 3rd platform added
- 20 posts/mo across all platforms
- 4 short-form videos/mo (Reels · TikTok · Shorts)
- AI content engine — 4 blogs or scripts/mo
- Basic web chatbot (FAQs + lead capture)
- Email marketing — 3 flows + 1 weekly campaign
- Monthly strategy call (30 min)
- Onboarding AED 1,500 one-time
- 3-month minimum

---

## Growth — AED 14,000/mo

Foundation plus paid acquisition, WhatsApp automation, cold email, influencer sourcing, and an analytics dashboard tying it all together.

**Best for:** ecom brands ready to scale paid ads aggressively, B2B teams wanting an outbound pipeline running daily, real-estate or clinic groups using WhatsApp as primary sales channel.

**What you get:** 4 platforms, 30 posts/mo, 10 short-form videos/mo, full Meta + TikTok ads, WhatsApp automation, 500 cold-email prospects/mo, 3 sourced creators/mo, analytics dashboard.

**Includes (everything in Foundation, plus):**

- 4th platform added
- 30 posts/mo + 10 short-form videos/mo
- Paid ads — Meta + TikTok (ad spend paid by client)
- WhatsApp Business AI automation
- Cold email lead gen — 500 prospects/mo
- Influencer + UGC sourcing — 3 creators/mo
- Analytics dashboard — all platforms in one view
- Bi-weekly strategy call (30 min)
- Onboarding AED 1,500 one-time
- 6-month minimum

---

## Scale — AED 32,000/mo

Full outsourced AI marketing department. Every platform, every service, voice agents, RAG assistants, TikTok Shop, SEO + GEO + AEO, plus one custom workflow build every quarter.

**Note: not sold before month 3 of relationship.**

**Best for:** funded scale-ups replacing a 4–6 person in-house team, multi-location groups running coordinated campaigns, established Dubai brands wanting one partner handling everything end-to-end.

**What you get:** all platforms including LinkedIn, Snapchat, and TikTok Shop. Unlimited posts. 20+ short-form videos/mo. AI SEO + GEO + AEO. Voice agents. RAG knowledge base assistant.

**Includes (everything in Growth, unlocked):**

- All platforms + LinkedIn + Snapchat + TikTok Shop
- Unlimited posts + 20+ short-form videos/mo
- SEO + GEO + AEO — Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini
- TikTok Shop management (listings + affiliates)
- 1 custom workflow automation build included
- Voice agents + RAG knowledge base assistant
- Dedicated strategist — single point of contact
- Weekly strategy call (45 min) + quarterly review
- Onboarding AED 1,500 (waivable for committed accounts)
- 12-month minimum (not sold before month 3)
