## Phase 1 — Hire now

The first operating team. Built to clear the founder bottleneck on delivery and ops while keeping the cost base lean.

### Ops Lead — monthly retainer, no commission

Runs daily delivery, assigns work, first line on problems. Owns no individual service — owns the system that makes the services ship on time.

**Owns:** no individual service.

### Account Manager — retainer + small commission

Owns the client relationship from signed contract through monthly review. Only voice the client talks to about scope, schedule, or escalation.

**Owns:**

- CRM Setup & Management
- PR & Press Releases

### Content Operator (1–2 people) — base + commission

Ships the content that goes on client accounts. AI-assisted production across social, captions, images, daily content engines.

**Owns:**

- Social Media Management — 1 Platform
- Social Media Management — 2 Platforms
- Social Media Management — 4 Platforms
- Daily Content Engine
- AI Content Engine
- AI Image Generation
- Personal Branding Management
- Arabic Content Creation
- Snapchat Management
- Community Management
- TikTok Shop Management
- Google Business Profile Optimization

### Video Editor / Producer — base + commission (or per-batch)

Owns all video output and live-stream production. Edits long-form, cuts shorts, produces the AI-generated product photography batches, and runs live shopping streams end-to-end.

**Owns:**

- UGC / Short-Form Video Ads
- Long-Form Video Editing + Clips
- AI Product Photography
- TikTok Live / Instagram Live Management

### Editor / QC — salary only, no commission

Final review on every deliverable before it touches a client. No individual service ownership — touches everything that ships.

**Owns:** no individual service. Reviews all output.
