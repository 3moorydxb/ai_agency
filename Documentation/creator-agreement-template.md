## 6. Confidentiality
Creator agrees not to disclose any client information, internal Nova process, prices, or strategy to any third party. This obligation survives termination of this agreement indefinitely.

## 7. No client poaching
For 12 months after the final delivery, Creator may not solicit, contract with, or accept work from any Nova client introduced through this engagement. Direct contact (DM, email, in-person) for the purpose of soliciting work is prohibited.

## 8. Non-disparagement
Creator agrees not to publicly disparage Nova Agency or any Nova client.

## 9. Termination
Either party may terminate with 7 days written notice for material breach (uncured after 3 days notice). Upon termination, Nova retains rights to any work delivered to date; Creator is paid pro-rata for accepted deliverables.
