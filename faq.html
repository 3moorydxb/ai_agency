AI & Technology

AI marketing is using machine learning models to do the work that used to require a team — content generation, ad creative variation, customer replies, lead qualification, and analytics. The output still needs human judgement, but the production cost drops 5–10x. That's what lets Nova price Foundation at AED 6,000/mo when an in-house equivalent costs AED 12,000+.
GEO is the practice of structuring your website so generative AI engines — ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews — cite you when users ask about your industry. It uses schema markup, FAQ structures, and clear factual content. Traditional SEO ranks you on Google's blue links; GEO gets you into the AI-generated answers people read instead.
AEO is optimizing for "answer engines" — featured snippets, voice assistants, and AI chat results. The technique overlaps with GEO but focuses on direct-answer formatting: short paragraphs, structured Q&A, schema.org FAQPage markup, and lists. Nova bundles SEO + GEO + AEO at AED 4,000/mo because all three signal types matter now.
AI helps with social media in three ways: generating post variations at scale, writing comment replies in your brand voice, and analysing which post types convert. The human still picks the angle and approves the final post — but the time per post drops from 30 minutes to about 4. That's the math behind multi-platform packages at AED 3,000–6,000/mo.
AI can replace about 70% of the work a junior social media manager does — drafting, scheduling, basic engagement, monthly reports. It cannot replace the strategic judgement of an experienced manager or the relationships needed for partnerships and PR. Nova's setup is AI plus one human reviewing every batch — never AI alone.
A WhatsApp chatbot is an automated agent that replies to messages on your WhatsApp Business account using AI. It can answer FAQs, book appointments, qualify leads, send catalogue links, and hand off to a human when needed. In the UAE, WhatsApp has roughly a 98% open rate — making chatbots one of the highest-ROI investments a Dubai business can make. See Chatbot build.
A RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) knowledge base lets an AI assistant answer questions using your own documents — pricing PDFs, SOPs, product specs — instead of inventing answers. Your docs are embedded into a vector database, the AI retrieves the most relevant chunks for each question, and generates an answer grounded in your real content. Nova builds RAG assistants from AED 9,000. See RAG Assistant.
Voice AI is a phone agent that can answer calls, take bookings, qualify leads, or run outbound campaigns — using a synthetic voice trained on your brand. Modern voice agents handle 60–80% of inbound queries without a human, freeing your team for the calls that actually need them. Nova builds voice agents from AED 8,000. See Voice Agent build.
Workflow automation connects your tools — CRM, email, WhatsApp, calendars, payment processors — so a single event (new lead, paid invoice, abandoned cart) triggers a chain of actions automatically. Nova uses n8n, Make, and Zapier to build flows that save 20–40 hours a week for most teams. See Workflow Automation build.
AI content generation works by giving a large language model your brand voice (a few past posts and a brief), then prompting it to produce variations on a topic. Nova's system runs each draft through three review passes: factual accuracy, brand voice, and platform-specific length. The human still picks the winner. Nothing ships unedited.
AI-generated content is fine for SEO as long as it's factually accurate, original, and reviewed by a human. Google's policy is about helpfulness, not authorship. Cheap, unedited AI content gets demoted — but well-edited AI content backed by clear schema markup tends to rank well in both Google and AI search engines.
