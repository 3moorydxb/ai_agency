Cookie Policy

A short explainer on the cookies novaagency.me places on your device and how to control them.

Last updated: 30 May 2026 Effective: 30 May 2026 Governing law: UAE / DIFC
01 · What they are

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, optimise performance, and provide analytical data to site owners.

02 · How we use them

How Nova Agency uses cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies divided into the following categories:

Essential cookies

Required for core website functions, security validation, and maintaining your chat state when interacting with our Llama 3.1 8B concierge widget.

Analytical cookies

Help us evaluate traffic patterns, identify performance bottlenecks, and monitor how visitors move through our digital content.

Preference cookies

Remember your language choice (English / Arabic) and cookie-banner decision so you don't see the banner on every visit.

03 · Your choices

Managing your cookie choices

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

For on-site control, use our cookie consent banner (shown on first visit) to select your processing preferences. To reset that choice, clear your site data for novaagency.me in your browser settings.

04 · Contact

Questions about cookies

Contact us at hello@novaagency.me or via the contact page. See also our Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Master Services Agreement.
