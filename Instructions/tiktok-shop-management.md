# TikTok Shop Management

## What the client gets
End-to-end TikTok Shop operation: product listings optimized for TikTok search, shoppable LIVE / video content, affiliate/creator outreach, order management oversight, and growth reporting. For ecom brands selling via TikTok in regions where TikTok Shop is available.

[NEEDS CLARIFICATION: TikTok Shop is not yet live in UAE/KSA as of writing. Confirm client geography — likely US, UK, SEA. If UAE-based client, this service may be for their export market only.]

## What Worker does — step by step

1. **Catalog setup — TikTok Seller Center.** Import products via CSV. For each: optimized title (keyword-rich, under 60 chars), 5+ images (white-bg required), 1+ video, description with bullets, variants, inventory, shipping template. Use Claude.ai to draft:
   > "Write a TikTok Shop product listing for {{PRODUCT}}. Output: title (60 char max, include {{KEYWORD}}), 5 bullet benefits, full description (200 words, conversion-focused, casual TikTok voice), 10 search keywords. Plus 3 video angle ideas for the product page video."

2. **Shoppable video content — feeds TikTok Shop algorithm.** Each product needs at least 3 organic TikTok videos tagging the product. Use `ugc-short-form-video-ads.md` workflow, with one extra step: tag the product in TikTok before posting.

3. **Affiliate / creator outreach — TikTok Affiliate Center + Claude.ai.** Weekly:
   > "Find 20 TikTok creators in {{NICHE}} with 5K-100K followers, high engagement, and posts that match {{BRAND_AESTHETIC}}. For each: handle, follower count, why fit, commission to offer, draft DM message."
   Worker sends invites via the Affiliate Center. Approve creators. Provide samples to top fits.

4. **LIVE shopping events — monthly.** Brief client or creator host. Worker preps:
   - Script outline (Claude.ai): hook, product demos, discount drops, CTAs
   - Product queue in the LIVE dashboard
   - Promo codes set
   - Promotion plan: tease 3 days before, go live, recap clip after

5. **Order ops oversight — daily check.** Confirm all orders fulfilled within SLA. Escalate stockouts, refund disputes, negative reviews to the client.

6. **Monthly report — Claude.ai.** GMV, ROAS, top SKUs, affiliate performance, return rate, content performance. Recommendations for next month's catalog and content focus.

## Tools used
- TikTok Seller Center — catalog, orders, ads
- TikTok Affiliate Center — creator program
- Claude.ai Project — listing copy, outreach, scripts, reports
- CapCut Pro — shoppable video edits

## Time required
- Catalog setup: 6-10 hours one-time (depends on SKU count)
- Weekly: 8-10 hours (content + affiliate + LIVE prep)
- Monthly LIVE: 4 hours
- Reporting: 1.5 hours
- **Total: ~40 hours/month**

## What to send the client
- Shoppable videos posted (visible in their feed)
- Affiliate dashboard access
- Monthly report with GMV breakdown

## Quality check - CTO & COO review
- Product titles have search keywords without keyword-stuffing
- All product images meet TikTok's policy (no text overlays, no logos in main image)
- LIVE scripts have hook, demo, urgency, CTA
- Affiliate commissions priced competitively (typical 10-30%)
