# Influencer & UGC Sourcing (3 Creators/mo)

## What the client gets
Three vetted creators sourced, briefed, and managed per month. Each creator produces content (UGC video, post, or campaign) that the client owns and can use organically or in paid ads. Includes negotiation, contracts, content review, and payment processing oversight.

## What Worker does — step by step

1. **Creator brief — Claude.ai.** Prompt:
   > "Write a creator brief for {{CLIENT_NAME}}'s campaign. Goal: {{GOAL}}. Product: {{PRODUCT}}. Deliverable: {{TYPE}} (e.g. 1x 30s UGC video for paid ads, no music required). Talking points: {{POINTS}}. Do/don't list. Usage rights requested: {{RIGHTS}}. Budget per creator: AED {{BUDGET}}. Timeline."

2. **Creator sourcing — manual + AI assist:**
   - **UGC marketplaces:** Insense, Billo, Trend, JoinBrands — fast, vetted, paid
   - **Direct outreach:** Modash, HypeAuditor, or manual Instagram/TikTok search
   - **Claude.ai prompt for direct sourcing:**
     > "I need to find UGC creators for {{NICHE}} in {{REGION}}. Build a list of 30 candidates with: handle, follower count, engagement rate, content style match (1-10), recent brand partnerships visible. Filter for authentic-looking content (not over-produced)."

3. **Outreach — Claude.ai.** Per creator:
   > "Write a DM/email to {{CREATOR_HANDLE}} offering {{CLIENT_NAME}}'s {{CAMPAIGN}}. Personalize off their recent {{POST_OR_VIDEO}}. Lead with what's in it for them. Mention rate transparently. Attach brief."

4. **Negotiation + contract — DocuSign or PandaDoc.** Standard creator agreement (Nova should have a template — [NEEDS CLARIFICATION: confirm Nova has a creator agreement template; if not, draft one with a UAE legal review.]). Cover: deliverables, deadline, usage rights, exclusivity window, payment terms, kill fee.

5. **Brief delivery + check-in — Notion or Loom.** Send creator the brief in Loom format (more clicks-through). Check in midway through production to catch off-brand drift early.

6. **Content review — Claude.ai (and human).** When creator delivers:
   > "Score this content against the brief. Does it hit: hook, product placement, talking points, CTA, brand voice, technical quality? Flag any deviations. If acceptable, draft approval message. If not, draft a revision request — be specific and kind."

7. **Payment processing — escrow or post-approval.** Confirm payment with client. Worker triggers payment via the marketplace or wire. Track in a master sheet.

8. **Usage — hand off to paid ads pipeline (`paid-ads-management.md`) or organic schedule.**

9. **Monthly report — creator list, content delivered, performance if used in ads, learnings.

## Tools used
- Insense / Billo / Trend / JoinBrands — UGC marketplaces
- Modash / HypeAuditor — direct sourcing data
- Claude.ai Project — briefs, outreach, content review
- DocuSign / PandaDoc — contracts
- Notion or Sheets — creator CRM / pipeline tracker

## Time required
- Per creator (sourcing → delivery): 2-4 hours
- 3 creators/month: 8-12 hours
- Reporting: 1 hour
- **Total: ~10-14 hours/month**

## What to send the client
- Creator profiles for approval before booking
- Content delivered to a Drive folder
- Monthly Doc: creators booked, content delivered, performance

## Quality check - CTO & COO review
- Creator fits brand aesthetic (not just follower count)
- Content matches brief — no scope creep accepted without re-pricing
- Usage rights signed before payment release
- Negative-result (low engagement) creators not re-booked
- Payment timing matches contract — no late pays

Growth, Scale. Standalone add-on AED 2,000/mo (creator payments are separate).
