# Build: Brand Identity & Design System

## What the client gets
A full brand identity package: logo (primary + variants), color palette (with hex/RGB/CMYK), typography system, voice & tone guide, visual style direction, social media templates, business card / pitch deck templates, and brand guidelines PDF. Build price AED 5,000–18,000. No ongoing maintenance.

## What Worker does — step by step

1. **Brand discovery — Claude.ai + client interview.** Prompt:
   > "Run a brand discovery for {{CLIENT_NAME}}. Cover: target customer (demographic + psychographic), positioning vs. competitors, brand archetype (Caregiver/Magician/Hero/etc.), 3 adjectives that describe the brand, 3 adjectives it must avoid, vibes/references they love, vibes they reject. Output as `BRAND_BRIEF.md`."

2. **Moodboard — Midjourney + Pinterest curation.** Generate 30+ on-direction visuals. Curate into 3 distinct moodboard directions for client to pick from.

3. **Logo concepts — Midjourney for ideation + Illustrator/Figma for execution.** Generate 8-12 directions via Midjourney prompts:
   > "Logomark concept for {{BRAND_NAME}}, {{NICHE}}. Style: {{AESTHETIC}}. Minimalist, monogram or icon, vector-ready. Output: white on black background, single concept, no text initially."
   Top 3 concepts → vector-execute in Illustrator/Figma. Refine.

4. **Color palette — Claude.ai + Coolors.** Prompt:
   > "Design a brand color palette for {{BRAND}} that reflects {{ADJECTIVES}}. Output: primary (1), secondary (1), 3 accents, 2 neutrals. Include hex, RGB, CMYK, Pantone-nearest, and use-case per color (background / heading / CTA / body / etc.). Plus accessibility notes (WCAG AA contrast against the others)."

5. **Typography — pair selection.** Heading + body font pair from Google Fonts (default) or premium foundries if budget. Claude.ai recommends pairings; worker tests in actual layouts.

6. **Voice & tone guide — Claude.ai.** Prompt:
   > "Write a voice & tone guide for {{BRAND}}. Include: voice persona (1 paragraph), 5 do's and 5 don'ts, vocabulary the brand uses, vocabulary it avoids, tone adjustments per context (web copy / social caption / customer support / legal). Plus 3 examples of an on-brand sentence and 3 off-brand rewrites."

7. **Templates — Figma / Canva.**
   - Social post template (3 variants)
   - Story template (2 variants)
   - Email header
   - Business card
   - Pitch deck (10-15 slides)
   - Letterhead
   - Invoice
   - Favicon, OG image

8. **Brand guidelines PDF — 15-25 pages.** Use Figma or Canva. Cover: logo usage (do's + don'ts), color palette, typography, voice & tone, social templates, image style, sample applications.

9. **Asset delivery — organized Drive folder.** Logos in every format (SVG, PNG transparent at multiple sizes, JPG, AI/Figma source). Color palette as ASE for Adobe + JSON. Fonts (or font links if web fonts).

10. **Handoff session — 30-min walkthrough.**

## Tools used
- Midjourney — moodboards + logo ideation
- Illustrator or Figma — logo execution
- Coolors / Adobe Color — palette
- Google Fonts / Fontstand — typography
- Canva or Figma — templates + guidelines doc
- Claude.ai — brief, voice & tone, palette logic

## Time required
- Discovery + moodboard: 6-10 hours
- Logo design + iteration: 15-25 hours
- Palette + typography: 4-6 hours
- Templates: 10-15 hours
- Guidelines doc: 6-10 hours
- **Total: 40-65 hours**

## What to send the client
- Discovery doc for approval (signed before design starts)
- 3 moodboard directions to choose from
- 3 final logo concepts to choose from
- Final brand guidelines PDF
- Organized Drive folder with all source + export files
- Loom walkthrough

## Quality check - CTO & COO review
- Logo works at favicon size AND billboard size
- Color palette passes WCAG AA contrast for text-on-background
- Templates editable by client without designer
- All files in working source format (not just PNG)
- Guidelines doc is actually usable — read it back, can someone unfamiliar create on-brand content?
- No stock or generic assets passing as bespoke
