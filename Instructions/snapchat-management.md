# Snapchat Management

## What the client gets
Daily Snap stories, Spotlight clips, and DM management on Snapchat. Targeted for GCC consumer brands where Snapchat still has heavy reach (especially KSA, UAE). Includes Snap Ads creative production if running paid.

## What Worker does — step by step

1. **Account setup & lens audit — once.** Worker reviews the brand's Snap Business account, confirms Spotlight eligibility, and lists relevant AR lenses to use or commission.

2. **Daily Snap stories — Claude.ai + phone.** Each morning, Claude generates the day's story arc:
   > "Plan {{CLIENT_NAME}}'s Snap story for today (5-8 snaps). Mix: behind-the-scenes, product, customer/UGC, poll or question, CTA. Each snap: visual brief (1 line), sticker/emoji overlay copy, swipe-up destination if applicable. Tone: casual, Snap-native, no marketing speak."
   Worker captures the snaps on phone (or uses Snap's web/desktop tools), adds text overlays per brief, posts.

3. **Spotlight clips (Snap's TikTok equivalent) — 3-5/week.** Reuse short-form video from `ugc-short-form-video-ads.md`. Snap rewards original content — adjust hook to mention "this Snap" instead of "this video" if needed.

4. **DM responses — Claude.ai, twice daily.** Same flow as social-media-management: paste DMs in Claude, generate brand-voice replies, paste back. Flag leads.

5. **Snap Map presence (for local businesses) — once + monthly.** Set up location pin, enable Snap Map check-ins, encourage UGC tagging.

6. **Snap Ads creative (if client runs paid) — see `ai-creative-ads.md`.** Snap-specific: 9:16, sound-on, first 2 seconds must hook, end card with clear CTA.

7. **Monthly report — Claude.ai.** Story views, screenshots, swipe-ups, Spotlight views, DM volume, leads generated.

## Tools used
- Snap Business Manager — account, ads
- Snap mobile app — daily stories
- Claude.ai Project — planning, copy, DM replies, report
- CapCut Pro — Spotlight edits

## Time required
- Setup: 2 hours one-time
- Daily stories: 30-45 min/day = ~17 hours/month
- Spotlight: 2 hours/week
- DMs: 30 min/day
- Reporting: 1 hour/month
- **Total: ~30-35 hours/month**

## What to send the client
- Confirmation snaps go live daily (they can verify in their account)
- Monthly performance Doc
- Lead alerts within 24 hours

## Quality check - CTO & COO review
- Snaps feel native, not recycled IG content
- Text overlays use Snap's font conventions
- Spotlight clips have hook at 0:00
- DM voice consistent with brand
